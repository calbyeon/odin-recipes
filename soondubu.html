Soondubu Jigae

Description

Soondubu Jigae is Korean for "Soft Tofu Stew". It is a savory silken stew that is perfect for rainy days. This is the first Korean recipe I tried to veganize on my own. It is very easy to veganize because all you need to remove is egg and meat. You can get some of the savory flavor by adding either mushroom buillion, or MSG, or both!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Vegan Kimchi
  • 3 TBSP Mushroom Buillion or MSG
  • 2 TBSP Soy Sauce
  • 1 TBSP Sesame Oil
  • 1/2 Cup Gochujang
  • 2 8oz Packages Soft Tofu

Directions

  1. Saute Kimchi in stone pot until a little golden.
  2. Add 2 cups of water and the respective broth powder.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and bring to a boil.
