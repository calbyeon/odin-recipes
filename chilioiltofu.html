Liangban Doufu

Description

Liangban doufu is an easy to make, savory, and naturally vegan dish. The cuisine is chinese, and is consumed as a summer dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 TBSP Soy Sauce
  • 2 TBSP Chili Oil (Laoganma)
  • 1 TSP Sesame Oil
  • 2 TBSP Minced Garlic
  • 2 TBSP Scallion
  • 2 8oz Packages Medium Tofu

Directions

  1. Mix all the ingredients together, but set aside half of garlic and scallions.
  2. Slice or cube tofu and lay it in your bowl.
  3. Pour sauce over the tofu, and top with the rest of the garlic and scallions.
